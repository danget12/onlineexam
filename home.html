General Directions

Welcome to the MoE Exit Exam. Please read the following instructions carefully before starting your exam:

  • The exam duration is 30 minutes.
  • Each question carries equal marks.
  • Do not refresh or close the browser during the exam.
  • Click "Submit" only after answering all questions.
  • Once submitted, you cannot change your answers.

When you are ready, click on My Exam to start.
